#|| Swaminarayan Aarti ||
Jai sadguru swami, (prabhu) jai sadguru swami,
Sahajanand dayalu, balvant bahunami...
Jai sadguru swami…

Charansaroj tamara, vandu kar jodi,
Charane chitt dharyathi, dukh nakhya todi...
Jai sadguru swami…

Narayan sukhdata, dvijkul tanudhari,
Pamar patit uddharya, aganit narnaari...
Jai sadguru swami…

Nitya nitya nautam leela, karta avinashi,
Adasath tirth charane, koti gaya kashi...
Jai sadguru swami…

Purushottam pragatnu, je darshan karshe,
Kaal karmathi chhuti, kutumb sahit tarashe...
Jai sadguru swami…

Aa avsar karunanidhi, karuna bahu kidhi,
Muktanand kahe mukti, sugam kari sidhi...
Jai sadguru swami…

Jai sadguru swami, (prabhu) jai sadguru swami,
Sahajanand dayalu, balvant bahunami...
Jai sadguru swami…
