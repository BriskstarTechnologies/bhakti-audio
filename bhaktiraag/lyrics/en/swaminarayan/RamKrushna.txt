#|| Ram Krishna Govind Dhun ||
Ram Krishna Govind, Jai Jai Govind!
Hare Ram Govind, Jai Jai Govind! (1)

Narayan Hare, Swaminarayan Hare!
Swaminarayan Hare, Swaminarayan Hare! (2)

Krishnadev Hare, Jai Jai Krishnadev Hare!
Jai Jai Krishnadev Hare, Jai Jai Krishnadev Hare! (3)

Vasudev Hare, Jai Jai Vasudev Hare!
Jai Jai Vasudev Hare, Jai Jai Vasudev Hare! (4)

Vasudev Govind, Jai Jai Vasudev Govind!
Jai Jai Vasudev Govind, Jai Jai Vasudev Govind! (5)

Radhe Govind, Jai Radhe Govind!
Vrindavanchandra, Jai Radhe Govind! (6)

Madhav Mukund, Jai Madhav Mukund!
Anandkand, Jai Madhav Mukund! (7)

Swaminarayan! Swaminarayan! Swaminarayan!
Swaminarayan! Swaminarayan! Swaminarayan!
