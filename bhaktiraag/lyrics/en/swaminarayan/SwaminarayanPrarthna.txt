#|| Swaminarayan Prarthana ||
Vishwesh cho sakal vishw tana vidhata
Traata tume sakal mangal shantidata
Maate tumharo karunanidhi satya naam
Sashtang naath tumne karu chu pranam...

Ajnaanpaash karuna kari kaati rakho
Nitye prabhu tav pade mam vrutti rakho
Bhaktonu paalan karo prabhu sarv yaam
Sashtang naath tumne karu chu pranam...

Je utpatti tatha sthiti lay kare, vedo stuti uchchare
Jena rom suchhidrama anusama, brahmand koti phare
Maya kaal ravi shashi surgano, aagya na lope kshan
Eva akshardhamna adhipati, Shree Swaminarayan...

Aavi akshardhamthi avnima, je dehdhari thaya
Aapya sukh apar bhaktjane, dile dharine daya
Kidhan charu charitra gaan karva, jene karuna kari
Vandun mangal murti ur dhari, sarvopari Shrihari...

Janmya Kaushal desh vesh batuno, lai teerthmahi farya
Ramanand malya swadharm chalvyo, yajnaadi mota karya
Mota dham rachya rahya Gadhpure, be desh gadi kari
Antardhan thaya leela haritani, sankship ma uchchari...

Tvameva mata cha pita tvameva, tvameva bandhuscha sakha tvameva।
Tvameva vidya dravinam tvameva, tvameva sarvam mam dev dev।।
Kayen vaachaa manasendriyarva, buddhyaatmana vaa prakritaih swabhaavat।
Karomi yadyatsakalam parasmai, Narayanayeti samarpyaami।।

Nijashritana sakalartihanta, sadarmbhakteravanam vidhata।
Data sukhana mansepsitana, tanotu Krushno’khilamangalam na।।
