#|| Jivu Chu Rasila ||
Jivu chu rasila tara mukhda ne hoti
Mukhdu joine taru, mohyu maan maru

Pankhida ne chhalee, ane angdai aavi
Tari vaat sathe, mori jaan gaavi

Jivu chu rasila tara mukhda ne hoti
Mukhdu joine taru, mohyu maan maru

Dulha na deep jalya, saathe badha na geet
Tari aankh ma jhalke, prem na meet

Jivu chu rasila tara mukhda ne hoti
Mukhdu joine taru, mohyu maan maru

Man mane darshan aave, re sanam tari
Prem na bandhan ma, bandhilu chhu hari

Jivu chu rasila tara mukhda ne hoti
Mukhdu joine taru, mohyu maan maru
