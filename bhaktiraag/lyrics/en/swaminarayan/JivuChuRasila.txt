#|| Jivu Chu Rasila ||
Jivu chhu rasila tara mukhdaane joti,
Haidana haar pyara nathadinu moti…2
Jivu chhu rasila…

Mukhadu joine taaru man mohyu maaru,
Piyar saasariyu sarve thayu man khaaru…2
Jivu chhu rasila…

Adhar amrut paani thai hu to gheli,
Nishank thai chhu lajja lok keri meli…2
Jivu chhu rasila…

Natvar nirakhi tune, antar thare chhe,
Durijan lok gholya laajine mare chhe…2
Jivu chhu rasila…

Man karm vachane hu thai rahi raati,
Mukhada upar jaye brahmanand vaari,
Jivu chhu rasila……
