#|| Naman Hu Karu Vishwapal || 
Naman hu karu Vishwapal ne.
Kargari kahu Bhaktibal ne.
Araj mhari ur maan dharo,
Vipatti mhari Shri Hari haro.

Avar aashro mhare nathi,
Sarv jan ne shu kahu kathi.
Kathin kaal maa aap chho ghani,
Sukhad Shyamla Mukt na Mani.

Amarna pati aash tahari,
Rasik rakhjo laaj mhari.
Pranatpaal chho sarv na Prabhu,
Deendayal chho Vishv na Prabhu.

Vimal murti ur maan vaso,
Najar thi Prabhu door na thasho.
Adhik traas ne todi naakhjo,
Brahmanand ne paas rakhjo.

Jay Swaminarayan.
