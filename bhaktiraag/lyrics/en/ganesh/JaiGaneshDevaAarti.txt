#|| Jai Ganesh Deva Aarti ||
Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh Deva।
Maata jaaki Paarvati, Pita Mahadeva।।

Ekadanta dayaavanta, chaar bhujaadhaari।
Mastak sindoor sohe, mooshak vaahan savaari।।

Paan chadhe, phool chadhe, aur chadhe mewa।
Ladduvan ka bhog lage, sant karen seva।
Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh Deva।।

Andhe ko aankh det, kodhin ko kaaya।
Baanjhan ko putra det, nirdhan ko maaya।।

Sur Shyaam sharan aaye, safal keeje seva।
Maata jaaki Paarvati, Pita Mahadeva।
Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh Deva।।

Deenan ki laaj rakho, Shankar sukhdaati।
Kaamana ko poorn karo, Jai Balihaari।।

Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh Deva।
Maata jaaki Paarvati, Pita Mahadeva।
Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh, Jai Ganesh Deva।
Maata jaaki Paarvati, Pita Mahadeva।
