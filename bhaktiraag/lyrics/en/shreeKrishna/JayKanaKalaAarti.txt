#|| Jai Kanha Kala Aarti ||
Jai Kanha Kala, Prabhu Natvar Nandalaala
Meethi Morli Vaala (2) Gopina Pyaara… Prabhu Jai Kanha Kala

Kaamangara Kaan, Kaaman Kain Kidha, Prabhu Kaaman…
Maakhan Chori Mohan (2) Chitt Chori Lidha… Prabhu Jai Kanha Kala

Nand Yashoda Gher, Vaikunth Utaryu Prabhu Vaikunth…
Kaaliya Mardan Kidha (2) Gayone Chaari… Prabhu Jai Kanha Kala

Govardhanne Tolyo, Tachli Aangalie
Indrano Garv Utaaryo (2) Jai Jai Giridhari… Prabhu Jai Kanha Kala

Gun Tano Tuj Paar, Kem Nahi Aave Prabhu Keme…
Neti Ved Pukaare (2) Bhakto Gun Gaave… Prabhu Jai Kanha Kala
