#|| Radha Rani Lage ||
Yamunaji to kaari kaari Radha gori gori |
Vrindavan mein dhoom machaave Barsane ri chhori |
Vrajdhaam Radhaju ki rajdhaani laage ||

Kanha nit murli me tere sumre baarambaar |
Kotin roop dhare Manmohan, tau na paave paar |
Roop rang ki chhabeeli patraani laage ||

Na bhaave mane maakhan-misri, ab na koi mithai |
Mhari jeebadli ne bhaave ab to Radha naam malai |
Vrishbhaanu ki lali to guddhaani laage ||

Radha Radha naam ratat hai jo nar aathon yaam |
Tinki baadha door karat hai Radha Radha naam |
Radha naam mein safal zindagaani laage ||
