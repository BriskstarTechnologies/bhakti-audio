#|| Vishvambhari Stuti ||
Vishwambhari Akhil Vishw Tani Janeta
Vidya Dhari Vadanma Vasjo Vidhata
Durbuddhine Door Kari Sadbuddhi Aapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo ।

Bhoolo Padi Bhavane Bhataku Bhavani
Sooje Nahi Lagir Koi Disha Jawani
Bhaase Bhayankar Vali Man Na Utaapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Aa Rankne Ugarava Nathi Koi Aro
Janmand Chhu Janani Hu Grihi Baal Taaro
Na Shu Suno Bhagwati Shishu Na Vilaapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Maa Karm Janma Kathni Karata Vicharu
A Srushtima Tuj Vina Nathi Koi Maaru
Kone Kahuu Kathan Yog Tano Balapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Hun Kaam Krodh Mad Moh Thaki Chkelo
Aadambare Ati Ghano Madathi Bkelo
Doshon Thaki Dushit Na Kari Maaf Paapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Na Shashtrana Shravan Nu Payapan Kidhu
Na Mantra Ke Stuti Katha Nathi Kai Kidhu
Shraddha Dhari Nathi Kara Tava Naam Jaapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Re Re Bhavani Bahu Bhool Thayi Che Maari
A Zindagi Thayi Mane Atishe Aakaari
Dosho Prajaali Sagla Tava Chhaap Chhaapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Khaali Na Koi Sthal Che Vin Aap Dhaaro
Brahmandma Anu Anu Mah Vaas Taaro
Shaktin Maap Ganwa Agneeti Maapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Pape Prapanch Karwa Badhi Vaate Puro
Khotu Kharo Bhagwati Pan Hun Tamaru
Jadyandhkaar Door Sadbuddhi Aapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Sheekhe Sune Rasik Chandaj Ek Chitte
Tena Thaki Vividha Taap Taleek Chitte
Vaadhe Vishesh Vali Amba Tana Pratapoo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Shri Sadguru Charan Ma Rahiine Bhaju Chhu
Raatri Dine Bhagwati Tujne Bhaju Chhu
Sadbhakt Sevak Tana Paritap Chhaapo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।

Antar Vishe Adhik Urmi Tata Bhavani
Gaaun Stuti Tava Bale Namine Mrigani
Sansarna Sakal Rog Samool Kaampo
Maam Paahi Om Bhagwati Bhav Dukh Kaampo।
