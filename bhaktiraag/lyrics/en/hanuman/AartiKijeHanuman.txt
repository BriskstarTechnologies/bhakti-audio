Dusht dalan Raghunath kala ki
Aarti kije Hanuman lala ki

Jake bal se girvar kaanpe
Rog dosh jake nikat na jhaanke
Anjani putra maha baldaai
Santan ke prabhu sada sahaai
Aarti kije Hanuman lala ki

Devira Raghunath pathaaye
Lanka jaari Siya sudhi laaye
Lanka so kot samudra si khaai
Jaat Pavansut baar na laai
Lanka jaari asur sanhaare
Siyaram ji ke kaaj sanvaare
Aarti kije Hanuman lala ki

Lakshman murchhit pade sakaare
Laaye Sanjeevan praan ubaare
Paithi pataal tori jamkaare
Ahiravan ki bhuja ukhaare
Baayin bhuja asur dal maare
Daahine bhuja santjan taare
Aarti kije Hanuman lala ki

Sur nar muni jan aarti utaren
Jai Jai Jai Hanuman ucharen
Kanchan thaar kapoor lau chhaai
Aarti karat Anjana maai
Jo Hanuman ji ki aarti gaave
Basahin Baikunth param pad paave
