#|| Hanuman Stotram ||
Neeti praveen nighamagham shaastra buddhe
Raja dheeraj Raghunaayak mantri vaarya
Sindoor charit kalevar naishtikendra
Shri Ramdoot Hanuman har sankatam meh

Sitaanimit jaaradhootam bhoorikaastha
Prouatsaraney Kaakaashaay hathastra paugha
Nirdhaag dhaatu patihatak raajdhaane
Shri Ramdoot Hanuman har sankatam meh

Durvarya Raavan visarjit shaktighaat
Kanthasu Lakshman sukhaarat jeev valle
Dronachal nayan nandit Ram paksh
Shri Ramdoot Hanuman har sankatam meh

Ram gamokti taritaarit bandhva yog
Dukhabdhi magna Bharatarpit pari barhaa
Ramagri padma madhupi bhavadant ratnam
Shri Ramdoot Hanuman har sankatam meh

Dant aatma Kesari maha kapi raatiyaa
Bhaarya Anjani puruta phal putrabhav
Taarkshyo pamochitava purabal teevravegh
Shri Ramdoot Hanuman har sankatam meh

Naana bichaari kavi srishta savir kritya
Vidraavana ranasmikshan
Duhpradarsh rogaagnaa satsuta davitad mantrajap
Shri Ramdoot Hanuman har sankatam meh

Naanaam dhe paadakshuti maatraa tohapi
Ye Brahmaraakshas pishaach gan cha bhootahaa
Teh maari kaans cha sabhayam tyaapa yaanti satvam
Shri Ramdoot Hanuman har sankatam meh

Tvam bhakta maanasa sameepit poorti shakto
Dinasya durmadaas patan bhayartibhaaj
Ishtam mamaapi paripoorn poornakaam
Shri Ramdoot Hanuman har sankatam meh

Shri Kashtabhanjan Dev ki Jai
