#|| Hanuman Chalisa ||
Shri Guru charan saroj raj nij manu mukur sudhaari
Barnaun Raghubar bimal jasu jo daayak phal chari
Buddhi heen tanu jaanike sumirau Pavan Kumar
Bal buddhi vidya dehu mohi harahu kalesh vikaar

Om Om

Jai Hanuman gyaan gun saagar
Jai Kapees tihun lok ujaagar
Ram doot atulit bal dhaamaa
Anjani putra Pavansut naamaa

Mahabeer Bikram Bajrangi
Kumati nivaar sumati ke sangi
Kanchan baran biraaj subesa
Kanan kundal kunchit kesa

Haath bajra aur dhwaja biraajai
Kandhe moonj janeu saajai
Sankar suvan Kesari nandan
Tej prataap maha jag bandan

Vidya vaan guni ati chatur
Ram kaaj karibe ko aatur
Prabhu charitra sunibe ko rasia
Ram Lakhan Sita man basia

Sookshm roop dhari Siyahim dikhava
Bikat roop dhari Lanka jarava
Bheem roop dhari asur sanghaare
Ramchandra ke kaaj sanvaare

Laay Sanjivan Lakhan jiyaaye
Shri Raghubeer harashi ur laaye
Raghupati kinhi bahut badaai
Tum mam priya Bharat-hi sam bhai

Sahas badan tumharo jas gaave
As kahi Shripati kanth lagaave
Sanakaadik Brahmaadi munisa
Narad Sarad sahit Aheesa

Yam Kuber Digpaal jahaan te
Kavi kobid kahi sake kahan te
Tum upkaar Sugreevahi keenha
Ram milaay raajpad deenha

Tumharo mantra Vibheeshan maanaa
Lankeshwar bhaye sab jag jaana
Yug sahasra yojan par bhaanu
Leelyo taahi madhur phal jaanu

Prabhu mudrika meli mukh maahi
Jaladhi langhi gaye achraj naahi
Durgam kaaj jagat ke jete
Sugam anugrah tumhre tete

Ram dware tum rakhware
Hot na aagya binu paisaare
Sab sukh lahe tumhari sharna
Tum rakshak kaahu ko darna

Aapan tej samhaaro aapai
Teenon lok haank te kaampai
Bhoot pisaach nikat nahin aave
Mahabeer jab naam sunaave

Naasai rog harai sab peera
Japat nirantar Hanumat beera
Sankat se Hanuman chhudaave
Man kram bachan dhyaan jo laave

Sab par Ram tapasvee raaja
Tin ke kaaj sakal tum saaja
Aur manorath jo koi laave
Soi amit jeevan phal paave

Chaaron yug prataap tumhaara
Hai prasiddh jagat ujiyaara
Sadhu sant ke tum rakhwaare
Asur nikandan Ram dulaare

Ashta siddhi nav nidhi ke daata
As bar deen Janaki maata
Ram rasaayan tumhare paasaa
Sadaa raho Raghupati ke daasa

Tumhare bhajan Ram ko paave
Janm janm ke dukh bisraave
Ant kaal Raghubar pur jaai
Jahan janm Haribhakt kahaai

Aur devta chitt na dharai
Hanumat se hi sarv sukh karai
Sankat kate mite sab peera
Jo sumire Hanumat balbeera

Jai Jai Jai Hanuman Gosaaee
Kripaa karahu Gurudev ki naaee
Jo sat baar paath kar koi
Chhoothahi bandi maha sukh hoyi

Jo yeh padhe Hanuman Chaleesa
Hoy siddhi saakshi Gaurisaa
Tulsidas sada Hari cheraa
Keejai Naath hriday mein deraa

Pavansut sankat haran mangal moorti roop
Ram Lakhan Sita sahit hriday basahu Surbhoop

Mere Prabhu Ram Jai Jai Ram
Siyapati Ram Jai Jai Ram

Mere Prabhu Ram Jai Jai Ram
Om Jai Ram
