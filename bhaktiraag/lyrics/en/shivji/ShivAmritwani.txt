#|| Shiv Amritvani ||
Kalpataru punyatama,
Prem sudha Shiv naam
Hitkarak Sanjeevani,
Shiv chintan aviraam
Patik paawan jaise madhur,
Shiv rasan ke gholak
Bhakti ke hansa hi chuge,
Moti ye anmol
Jaise tanik suhaaga,
Sone ko chamkaaye
Shiv sumiran se aatma,
Adbhut nikhri jaaye
Jaise chandan vriksh ko,
Dasate nahin hai naag
Shiv bhakton ke chole ko,
Kabhi lage na daag

Om Namah Shivaya, Om Namah Shivaya!

Dayanidhi Bhuteshwar,
Shiv hai chatur sujaan
Kan kan bheetar hai base,
Neelkanth Bhagwaan
Chandrachood ke trinetra,
Uma pati vishvaas
Sharanagat ke ye sada,
Kaate sakal klesh
Shiv dwaare prapanch ka,
Chal nahin sakta khel
Aag aur paani ka,
Jaise hota nahin hai mel
Bhay bhanjan Nataraj hai,
Damru wale Naath
Shiv ka vandan jo kare,
Shiv hai unke saath

Om Namah Shivaya, Om Namah Shivaya!

Laakho Ashwamedh ho,
Sau Ganga snaan
Inse uttam hai kahi,
Shiv charanon ka dhyaan
Alakh Niranjan naad se,
Upje aatm gyaan
Bhatke ko rasta mile,
Mushkil ho aasaan
Amar gunon ki khaan hai,
Chit shuddhi Shiv jaap
Satsangati mein baith kar,
Karlo pashchaataap
Lingeshwar ke manan se,
Siddh ho jaate kaaj
Namah Shivaya ratta ja,
Shiv rakhenge laaj

Om Namah Shivaya Om Namah Shivaya!

Shiv charanon ko chhoone se,
Tan man paawan hoye
Shiv ke roop anoop ki,
Samta kare na koyi
Mahabali Mahadev hai,
Mahaprabhu Mahakaal
Asuraankhandan bhakt ki,
Peeda hare tatkaal
Sarv vyaapi Shiv Bhola,
Dharm roop sukh kaaj
Amar Ananta Bhagwanta,
Jag ke paalan haar
Shiv karta sansaar ke,
Shiv srishti ke mool
Rom rom Shiv ramne do,
Shiv na jaiyo bhool

Om Namah Shivaya, Om Namah Shivaya!
